Le Wagon - Final project

The primary objective of the project was to develop a dynamic dashboard solution to facilitate comprehensive data tracking and streamline the sales process for AirInt. With a keen focus on addressing their challenges in acquiring new clients, our team of four embarked on a multifaceted approach aimed at extracting actionable insights and making predictive analyses.

Through this project, I honed my skills in data analytics, visualization, and predictive modeling, while also gaining invaluable experience in collaborating with teammates to tackle real-world business challenges.
